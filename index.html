About Viviana

An Ecuador native, Viviana has lived most of her life in the US, so is fluent in both Spanish and English. She minored in Spanish at O.S.U. while participating in a latino community center, and later worked as a Spanish GED assistant and instructor.

Viviana is a cheerful person who loves life and dancing salsa. She's a patient, encouraging teacher and loves working one-on-one to help people learn and then master Spanish.
